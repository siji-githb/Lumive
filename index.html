Frequently Asked Questions

What is Lumivé skincare all about?

Lumivé is a premium skincare brand committed to creating simple, luxurious, and effective products using carefully selected natural ingredients combined with advanced skincare science.

Can I use multiple products together?

Yes! Our products are designed to work beautifully together. We recommend starting with a cleanser, followed by a toner, serum, and moisturizer for a complete routine.

How do I know which products are right for me?

Take our Beauty Profile quiz above to receive personalized product recommendations based on your skin type, concerns, and skincare goals.

Are Lumivé products suitable for sensitive skin?

Absolutely. All Lumivé products are dermatologist tested, cruelty-free, and formulated to be gentle on all skin types, including sensitive skin.

How do I choose the right products?

Start by identifying your skin type and primary concerns. Our Beauty Profile form can help guide you, or explore our Best Sellers for our most-loved products.

Can I use these products daily?

Most of our products are designed for daily use. However, some treatments like our Overnight Renewal Mask are best used 2–3 times per week for optimal results.
